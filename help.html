General questions

How to register as our NGO partner? Is it free?

NGOs can register for free through our website. No fee is required as we are an open source platform intended to help NGOs.
Normal User Account

How to register for an event?

1. Login to your account.
2. Find an interested event in our events list or search for events based on your location.
3. Click on the register button and you will be automatically registered for the event.
4. A ticket which contains a QR code will be generated and will need to be presented when you attend the event.

How can I check my registered events?

All your registered events will be listed on the My events section of the webpage. You can view detailed information of the events or unregister from the events by clicking on the 'Unregister' button.
NGO account

How to create an event?

You can request to hold an event in the Event Request tab located on the side menu. Enter all the mandatory fields and submit the request.

Can I keep track of all my events?

Yes, this feature is available under the Event Schedule tab.

What is the approval process for events?

The steps involved are listed as below:
  • Login to your NGO account.
  • Submit a new event request
  • The event will be now placed under approval from the government.
  • Once the request is processed by the government, the request will be moved from the pending table to the approved/rejected table on the event schedule page.

Why should you join us?

Some reasons are:
  • Our platform is absolutely free of charge.
  • You can easily manage events during MCO.
  • You can keep track of your inventory easily
Government Account

How to approve/reject an event request?

1. Login to your government account.
2. Check out all the pending approval requests on the home page
3. Click on the request to view more information.
4. Approve or reject the request.

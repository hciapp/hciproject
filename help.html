NGO account

How to create a distribution event?

you could instantiate a distribution event in the MCO_request tab. Enter the mandotory information and you are ready to submit.

Can i keep track of active distribution event?

Yes, this feature is available under distribution_event tab.

How is the process of distribution event approval?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. Below are some vital steps:
  • Login to your NGO account
  • Submit a new distribution event request
  • The event will now under approval form governement.
  • Once the event is approved, you may now proceed with the distribution event.

How to approve a MCO request?

1. Login to your governemnt account.
2. Check out all pending approval request on the home page
3. Click on the request to view more detailed information
4. Approve or reject the request.
